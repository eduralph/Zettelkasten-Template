# 1 - Rough Notes

**Inbox / fleeting notes.** Raw capture — anything worth keeping, before it's processed.

## Purpose

- Zero-friction capture. Don't overthink the title, don't apply a template, don't link.
- Get the idea out of your head and into the vault.
- Process to a permanent note in [[6 - Zettelkasten/About|6 - Zettelkasten]] later — or delete if it didn't survive contact with time.

## Conventions

- No template required.
- No required frontmatter.
- Subfolders allowed if a theme of fleeting notes emerges (e.g., `Meetings/`, `Random/`) — but flat is fine.
- **Anything older than ~30 days here should be processed or deleted.** Stale inboxes become noise.
